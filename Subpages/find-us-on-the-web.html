Support That Feels Like Family

What sets Rose’s Agency Home Care apart is not just our dedication to high-quality service, but the way we care for each client as if they were our own family. From the moment we begin working together, we make it our mission to truly understand your lifestyle, health goals, and daily routines. That insight becomes the foundation for a personalized care plan designed to support your well-being physically, emotionally, and mentally.

Our hands-on care manager partners closely with each client and their family to coordinate everything from scheduling and insurance paperwork to managing your care team. You don’t need to worry about juggling logistics. We’re here to handle those details for you, so you can focus on living well.

Your Dedicated Care Team, Available 24/7

When you choose Rose’s Agency Home Care, you gain more than just a caregiver, you gain a dedicated team committed to your care, day and night. Our team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that support is always just a call away. Whether you need daily check-ins, overnight care, or emergency assistance, we are here for you whenever you need us.

This around-the-clock availability has earned us national recognition from Home Care Pulse, a respected leader in home care performance benchmarks. We are proud to be acknowledged for our commitment to client satisfaction and for consistently delivering care with integrity and heart.

A Personalized Experience Designed Around You

Every person is different, which means every care plan should be too. Our approach is simple: listen first, then act. Our care managers conduct thorough assessments and personal interviews to get to know each client’s needs, preferences, and lifestyle. This process helps us match you with a caregiver who not only meets your clinical and practical needs but also complements your personality and values.

Care plans include a wide range of services, including:

  • Companionship and conversation
  • Meal preparation and dietary assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Light housekeeping and laundry
  • Transportation to appointments
  • Assistance with personal hygiene and grooming
  • Specialized care for Alzheimer’s, dementia, or chronic conditions

And because needs change over time, our care managers remain involved throughout your care journey, making regular updates to your plan to keep it aligned with your health and preferences.

Training That Goes Beyond the Basics

We don’t just hire caregivers, we train and equip professionals who are ready to make a difference. Every caregiver at Rose’s Agency Home Care completes an extensive training process designed to meet and exceed California’s standards for home care aides. This includes:

  • Lessons in health and hygiene practices
  • Emergency response and safety protocols
  • Infection control procedures
  • Techniques for delivering compassionate personal care
  • Ongoing professional development through our Rose Care University

We carefully screen each applicant to ensure they possess not only the necessary skills but also the empathy and commitment that define our approach. Background checks are conducted through both the Department of Justice and nationwide databases. Once approved, caregivers are officially registered with the California Department of Social Services, giving families peace of mind that their loved one is in qualified hands.

Real Connections That Make a Difference

Our caregivers are more than just professionals, they’re companions, supporters, and often cherished members of the family. Raquel, one of our standout caregivers, shares what drives her every day: “I get to connect with people in a way that’s real. It’s not just about assisting with daily tasks, it’s about listening, sharing stories, and being there when someone needs you most. That’s what I love about caregiving.”

These genuine bonds are at the heart of what we do. Our clients consistently tell us how much their caregiver means to them, not just for the support they provide, but for the kindness and companionship they bring into their homes.

Why Families Trust Rose’s Agency Home Care

Choosing a home care provider is one of the most important decisions a family can make. At Rose’s Agency Home Care, we don’t take that trust lightly. Families choose us because we combine professionalism with heartfelt compassion. We are honored to be part of your story and will continue to uphold the highest standards in everything we do.

Here’s what makes us different:

  • Available 24/7 for your peace of mind
  • Personalized care plans tailored to you
  • Skilled, background-checked caregivers
  • Transparent communication with family members
  • National recognition for client satisfaction
  • A caregiving team that treats you like one of their own

If you or someone you love needs trusted in-home care, we invite you to reach out. Our team is here to listen, support, and guide you through every step of the journey. Let us help you create a care plan that brings comfort, dignity, and connection into your home. Contact Rose’s Agency Home Care today and experience care that feels like family.
